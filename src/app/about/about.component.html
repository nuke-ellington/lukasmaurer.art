Lukas Maurer is a fantasy illustrator specialized in traditional media.

Growing up in Munich, Lukas early discovered a deep fascination for the natural world of the past, present and future.
Once he came into contact with the works of fantasy artists like Les Edwards, Quinton Hoover and Uğurcan Yüce a life long passion for the genre was instantly ignited.
Nowadays he pulls inspiration from a variety of sources ranging from paleobiology to pop culture in order to create his work.

If he's not drawing or painting you can catch him play basketball or djing at a local venue.
